About Me

Where to begin? Being born and raised in Cleveland, Ohio, I thought i'd be immune to the cold; let's say i'd rather be in shorts and a tank over 3 jackets. Moving to Charlotte was not my idea, I didn't even have a say (I mean what 5 year old can make that decision!?). When I was young my dad would watch the market all day, and that made me really interested in that kind of risk. When I turned 16 my dad gave me my first $5,000 to invest and play around in the market. I became addicted to day trading penny stocks, I never felt a rush like that before. Many people would say that I am addicted to gambling, but I like to tell them that I am addicted to risk. Other then that I am a pretty normal guy that likes to cook and play basketball!
